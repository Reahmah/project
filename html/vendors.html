Our Team

Mr Hassan Sodiq (Customer Service Representative): These staff members assist customers with currency exchange transactions, provide information on exchange rates, and ensure a smooth service experience. Their friendly demeanor and excellent communication skills are crucial for customer satisfaction.

Compliance Officer: Compliance officers ensure that the bureau de change adheres to all regulatory requirements and anti-money laundering (AML) laws. They conduct regular audits, monitor transactions for suspicious activity, and maintain up-to-date records of all compliance-related documentation.

Forex Trader: Forex traders are responsible for buying and selling foreign currencies to ensure the bureau de change has sufficient stock of various currencies. They monitor market trends, analyze economic indicators, and make informed decisions to maximize profits and minimize risks.

Operations Manager: The operations manager oversees the daily operations of the bureau de change. They manage staff schedules, ensure compliance with company policies, handle customer complaints, and work to improve operational efficiency.

Cashier: Cashiers handle the actual exchange of currency. They count and verify the currency, process transactions, and ensure that all cash handling procedures are followed accurately. Attention to detail and strong numerical skills are essential for this role.

IT Specialist: IT specialists maintain the technological infrastructure of the bureau de change. They ensure that all software and hardware systems are functioning correctly, manage cybersecurity measures, and provide technical support to staff.
